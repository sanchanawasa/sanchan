## 🗼 What is Minato / 港区とは

**Minato (港区)** is one of Tokyo's most prestigious districts — home to Roppongi, Azabu-Juban, Shirokane, and Tokyo Tower. Known for luxury restaurants, rooftop bars, embassies, and an endless parade of expensive cars, Minato-ku is where Tokyo's wealthy elite gather to play.

**港区（みなとく）** は東京都心の高級エリア。六本木・麻布十番・白金・東京タワーを擁し、高級レストランやルーフトップバー、各国大使館が立ち並ぶ、東京随一のセレブ集積地。

## 💅 Who are 港区女子

**港区女子 (Minatoku-joshi)** is a Japanese cultural term for young women who frequent Minato ward's upscale nightlife scene — attending *gyara-nomi* (paid drinking parties), building connections with wealthy men, and navigating a glamorous but competitive social world.

Equal parts savvy, charming, and strategic — the 港区女子 knows exactly how to work a room.

**港区女子**とは、港区の高級ナイトシーンに生きる若い女性たちのこと。ギャラ飲みや交流パーティーに参加しながら、魅力と戦略で人脈を築いていく。華やかだが、その裏には計算と覚悟がある。

## 🎮 About the Game / ゲームについて

**Minato Quest** is a 5-minute browser RPG where you play as a 港区女子 trying to earn **¥1,000,000** before your 30th birthday.

**港クエスト**は5分で遊べるブラウザRPG。あなたは港区女子となり、30歳になる前に**100万円**を稼ぐことを目指す。

### How to Play / 遊び方

| | EN | JP |
|---|---|---|
| **Move** | Arrow keys / D-pad | 十字キー / Dパッド |
| **Talk** | Get close to an uncle → tap Talk | おじに近づいて「話しかける」 |
| **Enter** | Walk near a building → tap Enter | 建物に近づいて「入る」 |

### Key Features / 特徴

- **⭐ Gold Uncle** — A rare, ultra-fast uncle worth ¥500k–900k. Hard to catch. Hunt him down.
- **⚔️ HP Battle** — Drain the uncle's HP to zero in 3 turns to earn full payout
- **🏙️ Minato Map** — Walk around a pixel-art Minato ward: Roppongi Hills, Tokyo Tower, Azabu, Odaiba
- **💉 Cosmetic Clinic** — Spend ¥100k for a huge Charm boost (+45)
- **🌙 Ultimate Move** — "Makura" skill: all stats crash, but payout ×3
- **⏱ Age Pressure** — Time passes automatically. Hit ¥1M before you turn 30
